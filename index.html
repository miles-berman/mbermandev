My Work

Pocket Drums

A web-based drum machine, synth, and looper that allows anyone to create music!

Click icon to visit, or drag to bookmark

Using the web audio api, the machine has the ability to synthesize different drum sounds, synthesizer tones, and more. Audio file samples can also be loaded in, or recorded straight from the user's microphone. Use the built in algorithms for generating sequences and melodies, or plug in a MIDI keyboard to play the sounds just how you like them!
