City Views

City Views is a personal project I have been working on for quite a while. The idea behind this game was to create an experience where the player has very limited choices for movement and interaction in the world, and is forced to just sit and watch the world around them. I wanted to challenge myslef to create the game from scratch, working on all the music, art, animations, and game mechanics myself. It is a project I am very proud of, and demonstrates a lot of my skills, including:

  • Algorythmic Music Generation
  • Animation And Art Skills
  • C# Programming and Unity Development
  • Solo Game Development Skills

SMPL-HLPR

A python "sampler" to change bit depth, sample rate, and pitch of WAV, MP3, & M4A files. The app allows users to load files, make adjustments, and save the new samples. Users can adjust the bit depth, sample rate, and pitch of the audio, and apply these changes on the fly.

  • Python Programming
  • Audio Skills
  • UI and App Creation

An Intro To AI Using Natural Selection and Mutations

This paper I've written covers a somewhat abstract form of AI using natural selection, mutation, and a population of "organisms" to solve problems. The idea is that, simply through natural selection and mutation, a population of "organisms" can evolve to fit a multitude of problems. The paper is a great example of my writing and research skills, and demonstrates my understanding of AI and programming concepts.
